Web-dev adventure

15.12.2022

I had the idea of making my own website for a long time, but I never had the time or skills necessary to actually make it. After half a year of not having time for this I caught some lung sickness and had a week of free time to work on whatever I wanted instead of being stuck at school.

I fired up VS Code and began writing. First, I made the about me page then contact and then project list. The last page that had no content on it was the home page. I did not know what to add here for like a day or so. Then I saw AndnixSH and got inspired to write a blog.

This whole page is heavily inspired by Zusier from which I “borrowed” the CSS that makes those very cool looking lines. The rest is a mix of my own work and whatever I could find online (W3Schools). Believe it or not but I am really proud of this website and how it turned out. As a back-end developer I’m not good at making things look good but it turned out really well.
